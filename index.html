About me

Hi, I’m Parth Sharma, a passionate 3D artist specializing in hard surface modeling, animation, and 3D asset creation. Currently pursuing my bachelor's degree in Textile and Fibre Technology at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, I bring a blend of technical knowledge and artistic creativity to my work. As an active member of the Design Club at IIT Delhi, I collaborate with a vibrant community of designers to innovate and push creative boundaries.

Proficient in Blender and ZBrush, I excel in crafting detailed hard surface models, designing immersive environments, and creating 3D character models. My passion extends beyond traditional 3D artistry into the realms of CGI and VFX, where I am exploring new techniques to enhance my creative storytelling.

My notable projects include ARCANUM: The Mysteries of IIT Delhi Revealed, where I modeled intricate sci-fi tunnels, mysterious caves, and animated character movements, and the Rendezvous Marketing Animation Reel, a showcase of my ability to combine animation with engaging marketing visuals.

As a designer and artist, I’m driven by a passion for storytelling and the challenge of bringing bold, imaginative ideas to life. Whether it’s through realistic modeling, dynamic animations, or compelling narratives, I strive to create impactful visual experiences.
